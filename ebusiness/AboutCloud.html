What is a cloud

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics over the Internet. Companies offering these computing services are called cloud providers and typically charge for cloud computing services based on usage

Software as a Service (SaaS) - Salesforce

Software as a service is a way of delivering applicantions over the internet. These applications are sometimes called web-based software, on-demand software, or hosted software. By using a SaaS you can access your application anywhere over the internet, freeing space on your hardware and making your software easier to use.
